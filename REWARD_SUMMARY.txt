NOTE: These increases are LOGICAL because we removed 8 decimal places!
The actual REAL WORLD VALUE is similar:
- OLD: 0.01 O × 100M satoshis/O = 1M satoshis
- NEW: 1 O × 1 unit/O = 1 unit
- Both ≈ $1-2 USD value (assuming 1 O ≈ 1 liter water)

🎉 BENEFITS OF 0 DECIMALS

✅ Simplicity:        "You earned 1 O" not "0.01 O"
✅ Clarity:          No confusing satoshi conversion
✅ Physical Meaning: 1 O = 1 liter (direct 1:1)
✅ User Friendly:    Easy mental math
✅ Distinct:         Different from Bitcoin model
✅ Stable Coin:      Makes sense for non-volatile currency
