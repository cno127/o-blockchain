💯 DECIMAL SYSTEM

1 O coin     = 100 cents  (2 decimal places)
0.01 O       = 1 cent     (smallest unit)

Examples:
├─ Water costs $1.50    → 1.50 O  ✅
├─ Water costs $2.25    → 2.25 O  ✅
├─ Reward is 50.00 O    → 50.00 O ✅
└─ Fee is 0.10 O        → 0.10 O  ✅

WHY 2 DECIMALS (not 0 or 8)?
✅ Precision for water prices ($1.50, $2.75, etc.)
✅ Familiar (like USD cents, EUR cents)
✅ Simple (not 8 decimals like Bitcoin)
✅ Practical for stable coin
